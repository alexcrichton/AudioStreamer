AudioStreamerLogLevel

Log levels. Used to filter out certain levels of logging.

Each level down the chain towards verbose will include the previous log levels. For example, AS_LOG_LEVEL_WARN will include AS_LOG_LEVEL_ERROR and AS_LOG_LEVEL_FATAL.

See [AudioStreamer logLevel] for more information.
